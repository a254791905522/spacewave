Privacy Policy

Applies to: Breach Defense

We value your privacy. Breach Defense is an offline single-player game and does not require account registration or online matchmaking features.

1. Data Collection

  • We do not actively collect personal identity information such as your name, phone number, or email address.
  • We do not upload gameplay data from the app to developer-owned servers.
  • Game progress (such as level unlocks and star ratings) is stored locally on your device.

2. Permissions and Network

  • The game is designed to run without an active internet connection.
  • The app does not request sensitive permissions unrelated to core gameplay.

3. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe there is a related issue, please contact us via email below.
