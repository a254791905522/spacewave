Breach Defense

Breach Defense is a fast-paced offline arcade game where you guide a mini missile through dangerous terrain, tap to switch direction, dodge obstacles, and break through hostile defense zones.

Controls are simple, but clearing levels consistently and earning high star ratings requires timing, precision, and strong reflexes.

Key Features

  • One-tap direction switching: easy to learn, hard to master.
  • 50 auto-generated levels with progressively increasing difficulty.
  • Level unlocks, star ratings, and auto-saved progress.
  • Fully offline single-player gameplay.
  • 8-bit style BGM and SFX with audio toggle support.
